Info

Justus is a graphic designer specializing in publications and typography, with a strong interest in art direction and concept design. He has studied at Aalto University and the School of Visual Arts in New York. He is part of Noin Seitsemän a collective that won the Best Publication of the Year award at Vuoden Huiput, they were also shortlisted for Best Editorial and Student Designs at ADCE '24. These recognitions were for their work on three issues of the Tuli & Savu poetry magazine. Justus co-designed the issue Pyhä (3/2023) with Emma Tahvanainen. Currently, he is available for freelance projects.
