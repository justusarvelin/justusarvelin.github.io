Justus is a graphic designer working across publications and typography, with a strong interest in art direction and illustration. He studied Visual Communication Design at Aalto University and spent part of his Bachelor's degree at the School of Visual Arts in New York.

He currently works as a graphic designer and producer at Post Bar, and as a production graphic designer for Solstice Festival. His work has been recognized with the Vuoden Huiput award for Best Publication as part of a group of classmates and was nominated at ADCE ’24 in the editorial and student design categories for three issues of the poetry magazine Tuli & Savu.

In addition to commercial work, he is also working on a new community platform called No Commercial Potential, aiming to organize events, release music and whatnot...

Alongside graphic design, Justus organizes events and performs as a DJ. He is a resident at Post Bar and has played in recent years at Flow Festival and New York’s LOT Radio. All the guestmixes and recordings from his sets can be found on his SoundCloud. For booking inquiries, email: justus.arvelin@gmail.com
